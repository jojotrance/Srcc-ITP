We Provide The Best Education With Our Curriculum

SHS - STEM Strand

SRCCMSTHS offers the academic strand Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics for those who intend to pursue careers that are focuses on physical sciences, mathematics, engineering and technology. It includes specialized subjects namely General Physics, General Biology, Pre-Calculus, Basic Calculus and Research/Capstone Project.

SHS - ABM Strand

SRCCMSTHS offers the academic strand Accountancy, Business and Management to provide adequate pre-tertiary training for those who are inclined to pursue careers in accountancy, banking, entrepreneurship, finance and management in the corporate setting and in the tourism, and hotel and restaurant industries.

SHS - ICT-CSS Strand

SRCCMSTHS offers an Information and Communication Technology strand, specialized in Computer Systems Servicing NC II. The CSS NC II Qualification at TESDA consists of competencies that you must possess to be able to install and configure computer systems, set-up computer networks and servers, and to maintain and repair computer systems and networks.

This is how SRCCMSTHS started

On June 6, 2005 “SRCC” as our students call it, began its operation in providing quality education with emphasis on science & technology, mathematics and communication arts among deserving students of Taguig City and the Municipality of Pateros under the able leadership of its OIC/Principal, GEORGE P. TIZON, the concurrent Administrative Officer V of DepEd-Taguig City & Pateros.
The school took off and bent on soaring high to emulate its primary model of success, an ordinary public school student who became one of the greatest senators of the republic, the late Sen. RENATO “COMPAÑERO” CAYETANO. Indeed our students are proud to be labeled “Compañeros” and “Compañeras” of the Division of Taguig City and Pateros.
